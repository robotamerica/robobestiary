robobestiary

a provisional taxonomy of artificial beings. ongoing field notes.

filter:

autonomy level

non fixed script, no adaptation
intermittent automatic between human inputs
eventual learns; gradually reduces human need
full operates in perpetuity, no human required

body type

◈ hard — rigid, mechanical
◈ soft — flexible, deformable
◈ organic — biological materials
◈ none — software / virtual / no body

class

servant
guardian
companion
tool
sovereign
mirror
liminal

split border = multi-class specimen

i. servant

built for labour. their purpose is meant to precede their personhood, if it ever develops. these robots make us ask ourselves, at what point does a tool become an enslaved person?
